Analysis

To make the network displayed above, I first found the 5 nearest neighbors to each point (or compound) using a kdtree. I then linked each point to its nearest neighbors, regardless of whether or not it was a mutual relationship. Once I had this graph like structure, I was able to perform the pagerank algorithm to the graph, to determine the importance of each of the points. The relative importance of each of the points is marked by the size of the point, the bigger the point is represented on the network above.

You can more closely examine the network represented above by single clicking on a point to see its attributes and then double clicking on it to isolate that point and its connections. Once you isolate a point, you can then "focus" or double click on another node to see all of its connections, as well as the already focused node, and so on as you desire.
